    FAQ¶

    The OneSurvey FAQ addresses common questions about accounts, projects, surveys, media, and support. Click a question to expand the answer.

    General¶

    What is OneSurvey?

    OneSurvey streamlines the entire lifecycle of technology and security projects — covering site surveys, design, proposals, installation, and maintenance — inside one collaborative workspace.

    Which browsers are supported?

    Use the latest version of Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari. Older browsers may lack key features or display inconsistencies.

    Is there a mobile app?

    A lightweight mobile web experience is available, but for full functionality we recommend using a desktop browser or tablet.

    Account & Access¶

    How do I create an account?

    An organization administrator must invite you by email. Open the invitation and follow the “Join OneSurvey” link to set your password. See: Creating an Account.

    I never received an invitation. What should I do?

    Check your spam or junk folder first. If it’s not there, ask your admin to resend the invite or verify your email address.

    How do I reset my password?

    Click “Forgot Password” on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to you.

    Can I update my profile information?

    Yes. Open the profile dropdown and select Profile to update your name, avatar, or other personal details.

    Projects & Surveys¶

    How do I create a new project?

    Open the Projects section and click New Project. Fill in the project name, client, and optional metadata, then save. See: Create a Project.

    How do I upload a floor plan?

    In the survey workspace, select “Upload a Floor Plan” and pick a PDF or image file. The first page or image becomes the canvas background. See: Upload a Floor Plan.

    How do I set the scale of my floor plan?

    Double-click two points on the canvas to drop a ruler, enter the real-world distance (for example, “3 ft”), and the canvas will calibrate all measurements accordingly.

    Can I revert to an earlier survey layout?

    Yes. Open Version History from the survey toolbar, browse previous snapshots, and restore the version you need. See: Version History.

    Why is an element missing from the canvas?

    Check Visibility Filters to ensure the element’s category isn’t hidden, and verify you’re viewing the correct floor plan and version. See: Visibility Filters.

    Elements & Canvas¶

    How do I place elements on the canvas?

    Drag elements from the sidebar libraries and drop them onto the floor plan. See: Drag & Drop Elements.

    How do I change an element’s label or color?

    Select the element and use the mini popups:

    • Design — label text and positioning. See: Design & Labels.
    • Color & Styling — icon color or appearance. See: Color & Styling.
    Can I link photos or files to an element?

    Yes. Open Element Information for that element and use the Photos & Files section to upload or link media. See: Element Information & Metadata and Link Photos & Files.

    How do I document installation requirements?

    Use Installation Access in the element popups to record access needs, assignments, or special instructions. See: Installation Access Notes.

    Media & Reports¶

    Where can I view project photos?

    Project photos appear under Project Photos or within OneSnap for event-based collections. Albums group related images for easy sharing. See also: Project Albums.

    Can I attach files or documents to a project?

    Yes. Use the Attachments tab to upload drawings, spreadsheets, or other supporting files.

    How do I generate a report?

    Many tabs include a three-dot menu with “Save PDF Report” or “Save Spreadsheet Report.” After processing, the report appears in the Reports tab for download.

    Where do generated reports go?

    All exported reports — PDFs or spreadsheets — are stored in the Reports tab, where you can preview, download, or delete them.

    Organization & Permissions¶

    How do I invite new users?

    Admins can invite users from Organization → Users & Roles. Enter the user’s details, assign roles, and send the invite. See: Users & Roles.

    What do different roles allow?

    Roles such as Account Manager, Collaborator, Technician, or Viewer determine access levels for projects, surveys, and media. See: Users & Roles.

    How do I manage clients?

    Use Organization → Clients to add or edit client organizations, link projects, and manage client-specific notes. See: Clients.

    Support & Troubleshooting¶

    How do I report a bug or request a feature?

    If the Tickets module is enabled, log issues directly inside the app. Otherwise, contact your support team through established channels (email or help desk). See: Tickets.

    Where can I find more help?

    Visit the Glossary for terminology and explore the other pages in these docs. Your organization’s administrator can assist with account or permission issues.

    How do I clear cached data if something looks outdated?

    Open the profile dropdown and select “Clear Cache,” then refresh the page.
