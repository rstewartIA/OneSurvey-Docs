# Gallery

## Overview
Gallery is the site media workspace. Photos and albums are managed together here.

## All Photos View
In **All Photos**, teams can:
- Upload and review site photos.
- Search and filter photos.
- Select single or multiple photos.
- Add selected photos to a new or existing album.

Common filters include floor plan, element, category, user, stage, installation status, and date range.

## Albums View
In **Albums**, teams can:
- Create and rename albums.
- Open an album to review photos.
- Add or remove photos from an album.
- Use album groupings for review packages and handoff.

## Typical Workflow
1. Capture or upload photos.
2. Tag and filter to find what you need.
3. Group related photos into albums.
4. Use albums in reviews and reporting.
