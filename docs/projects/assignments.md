# Assignments

## Overview
Assignments track owned work from planning through verification.

## Where Assignments Appear
- **Site Detail > Assignments** for site-specific execution.
- **My Assignments** for cross-site work.

In **My Assignments**, users can switch between:
- **My Assignments** (work assigned to me).
- **Assignments I've Sent** (work I assigned to others).

## Common Statuses
- To Do
- In Progress
- Blocked
- Completed
- Verified

## Common Priorities
- Critical
- High
- Medium
- Low

## What Teams Update
- Owner/assignee.
- Due date.
- Status and priority.
- Comments and attachments.
- Linked survey and element context.
- Activity history.

## Typical Workflow
1. Create assignment and set owner/priority.
2. Link to the related survey or element when needed.
3. Update status as work moves.
4. Add comments and attachments during execution.
5. Mark completed and verify.
