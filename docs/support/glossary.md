# Glossary

**Assignment**: A tracked work item with owner, priority, status, comments, and history.

**Client**: A customer record used to group sites, OneSnap activity, and SLA settings.

**Gallery**: The site media area where photos and albums are managed together.

**Invitation**: Admin-sent access email used to join an organization and complete account setup.

**OneSnap**: A fast photo workflow for capture, albums, map review, and reporting.

**Owner**: The highest organization role with full administrative authority.

**Role**: Organization authority level: Owner, Manager, or Member.

**Seat**: Capability level in the app: Full, Field, or Viewer.

**Ticket**: A service or issue tracking item tied to site context.

**Version History**: Saved survey states that let teams review and restore earlier versions.
