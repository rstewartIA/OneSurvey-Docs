# FAQ

## Access and Login

<details>
  <summary>I did not receive my invite email. What should I do?</summary>
  <p>Check spam or junk first. If it is not there, ask your account manager or owner to resend your invitation from <strong>Users</strong>.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>My invitation link says expired. How do I continue?</summary>
  <p>Ask your account manager or owner to resend the invite. The new link will let you complete setup.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How do I reset my password?</summary>
  <p>From login, select <strong>Forgot Password</strong>, enter your email, and use the reset link sent to you.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Why do users see different menus?</summary>
  <p>Menus are based on role and seat. For example, Assignments and OneSnap visibility depends on your assigned access.</p>
</details>

## Site Workflow

<details>
  <summary>Where do I manage photos and albums for a site?</summary>
  <p>Use the site <strong>Gallery</strong> tab. Photos and albums are managed together there.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>When should I use Assignments vs Tickets?</summary>
  <p>Use <strong>Assignments</strong> for owned execution work and <strong>Tickets</strong> for service issues and support workflows.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Can a ticket become an assignment?</summary>
  <p>Yes. In ticket detail, use <strong>Convert to Assignment</strong> when work should move into execution tracking.</p>
</details>

## User Management

<details>
  <summary>Who can invite users?</summary>
  <p>Managers and owners can invite users. Only owners can assign the Owner role.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Why can I not assign a seat to a new user?</summary>
  <p>You may have reached a seat limit. Check seat usage on the Users page.</p>
</details>

## Mobile

<details>
  <summary>What can I do from mobile?</summary>
  <p>Open sites, update survey elements, upload photos, and manage assignments or tickets based on your access.</p>
</details>
