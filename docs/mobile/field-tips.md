# Field Tips

## Before You Arrive
- Confirm site access and assignment ownership.
- Confirm your device is charged.
- Confirm expected connectivity at site.

## While On Site
- Update statuses as work happens.
- Attach photos directly to the right element.
- Keep comments short and specific.
- Use assignments and tickets to keep ownership clear.

## Before You Leave
- Check for incomplete assignments.
- Confirm required photos were uploaded.
- Confirm any active issues are logged as tickets.
