# Mobile Overview

## Overview
OneSurvey mobile is designed for fast field updates and quick review.

## Main Navigation
Depending on access, mobile navigation can include:
- OneSurvey
- Assignments
- Tickets
- Notifications
- OneSnap
- Settings

## Access Behavior
- Assignments are available to Field and Full seats.
- Tickets and OneSnap are available to Viewer seat and above.
- Menu visibility follows each user's role and seat.

## Typical Field Workflow
1. Open a site.
2. Open a survey and update element details.
3. Capture photos.
4. Update assignments and tickets.
5. Check notifications before leaving site.
