# Clients

## Overview

Available to: **Manager, Owner**

Clients are customer records tied to sites, OneSnap sessions, and ticket service expectations.

## Client Detail Tabs

- **Basic Info**: client profile and contact details.
- **Sites**: linked sites for that client.
- **One Snaps**: linked OneSnap sessions.
- **SLA**: ticket service-level settings.

## Typical Workflow

1. Create or open a client record.
2. Confirm contact and profile details.
3. Link sites to the client.
4. Review OneSnap activity and SLA expectations.
