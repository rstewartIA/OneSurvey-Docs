# Audit Logs

## Overview
Audit Logs gives owners a searchable history of important changes across sites, users, assignments, and tickets.

## Who Can Use It
- Owner role.

## Common Filters
- Search text.
- Site.
- User.
- Record type.
- Event type.
- Source.
- Date range.

## What You Can Review
- Who made the change.
- What record was changed.
- When it happened.
- Field-level before/after values.

## Common Use Cases
- Confirm who changed a user's role or seat.
- Review assignment or ticket status changes.
- Validate site updates during handoff or QA.
