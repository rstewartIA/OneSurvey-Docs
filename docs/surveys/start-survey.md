# Start a Survey

## Overview
Start a survey from a site to begin floor plan planning and field documentation.

## Steps
1. Open a site.
2. Go to **Surveys**.
3. Select **Create Survey**.
4. Enter a survey name.
5. Open the survey workspace.

## Next Actions
- Upload and scale your floor plan.
- Place elements.
- Add photos and comments.
