## Canvas Workspace Basics

The canvas is the primary survey workspace for placing elements, adding context, and reviewing progress before handoff.

<div class="os-screenshot">
  <div class="os-screenshot-card">
    <img src="../assets/images/canvas-basics.png" alt="Survey canvas workspace">
  </div>
  <p class="os-screenshot-caption">Use the canvas to place elements and update details in context.</p>
</div>

### Core Actions
- Pan and zoom to the area you are documenting.
- Select, move, and update placed elements.
- Open the [Element Sidebar](design.md) to manage details, photos, and tickets.
- Switch floor plans or versions as needed.

<div class="os-screenshot">
  <div class="os-screenshot-card">
    <img src="../assets/images/scale-floorplan.png" alt="Floor plan scale controls">
  </div>
  <p class="os-screenshot-caption">Set floor-plan scale before detailed placement for accurate mapping.</p>
</div>

### Practical Workflow
1. Open the target floor plan.
2. Place or select elements on the canvas.
3. Update details in the [Element Sidebar](design.md).
4. Use [Visibility Filters](visibility-filters.md) to validate the final view.
