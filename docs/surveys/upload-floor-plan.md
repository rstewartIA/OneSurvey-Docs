# Upload a Floor Plan

## Overview
Upload the floor plan image or drawing used as the survey background.

<div class="os-screenshot">
  <div class="os-screenshot-card">
    <img src="../../assets/images/upload-floorplan.png" alt="Upload floor plan modal">
  </div>
  <p class="os-screenshot-caption">Upload a floor plan as the survey base layer.</p>
</div>

## Steps
1. Open the survey.
2. Select **Upload Floor Plan**.
3. Choose your file.
4. Confirm upload.
5. Set scale for accurate measurements.

<div class="os-screenshot">
  <div class="os-screenshot-card">
    <img src="../../assets/images/floorplan-upload-button.png" alt="Upload Floor Plan action button">
  </div>
  <p class="os-screenshot-caption">Use the Upload Floor Plan action from the survey toolbar.</p>
</div>

## Tips
- Use clear, high-quality files.
- Use clear names for multi-floor sites.
- Confirm scale before placing many elements.
